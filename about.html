Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015 by Jonathan Ambrose, Prime Dough started as a small home bakery with a passion for traditional techniques and quality ingredients. What began as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a beloved town-based bakery.

Today, we're proud to serve our community with the same dedication to craftsmanship that started it all.

Our Mission

At Prime Dough, we believe in baking with purpose. We're committed to:

  • Using only the finest organic, locally sourced ingredients
  • Preserving traditional baking methods while embracing innovation
  • Creating a warm, welcoming space for our community
  • Sustainable practices that honor our planet
